Personal Information

Dongming Han is now a Ph.D. student of the State Key Lab of CAD&CG and a member of Visual Analytics Group (VAG) (supervised by Prof. Wei Chen) at Zhejiang University. He got his B.S. in software engineering from Zhejiang University, China in 2017. His current research interests are Information Visualization, Visual Analytics and Graph Visualization.
